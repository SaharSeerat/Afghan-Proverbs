Afghan proverbs reflect centuries of wisdom, values, and culture. They are passed down from generation to generation and teach life lessons through short, powerful words.

با صبر، تُرش شیرین می‌شود

With patience, the sour turns sweet.

سنگ نرم را آب نرم می‌سازد

Soft water wears away hard stone.
